我的 AI 協作方法論My AI Collaboration Method

01

定義問題Define

先寫清楚需求與限制（需求文件、規則 PDF、JTBD），給 AI 的輸入品質決定輸出品質。Write clear requirements and constraints first (spec docs, rule PDFs, JTBD). Input quality determines output quality.

02

拆解任務Decompose

把大目標切成 AI 一次能做好的模組，逐步推進而非一次到位。Break the goal into modules AI can handle in one pass. Incremental progress, not big-bang delivery.

03

迭代審查Iterate & Review

每一版人工審查、提出領域約束讓 AI 修正。cellDB 的 v1→v6 演進就是這個循環的紀錄。Review each version manually and surface domain constraints for AI to fix. The v1→v6 cellDB evolution is this loop on record.

04

驗證成果Verify

用測試、模擬與實際 demo 驗證 AI 產出：約束測試、3M 次模擬、遠端 demo 都是驗證手段。Validate AI output with tests, simulations, and live demos: constraint tests, 3M simulations, and remote demos are all verification methods.
